Security Audit & Compliance Review: Botium Toys

Role: Cybersecurity Analyst

Objective: To conduct a comprehensive security audit of Botium Toys' IT infrastructure, identify vulnerabilities, and assess adherence to PCI DSS, GDPR, and SOC 2.

Scope:

  • Evaluation of company-wide IT assets (hardware, software, network systems)
  • Review of implemented security controls
  • Assessment of compliance with regulatory standards
  • Risk scoring and prioritization

What I Did:

  • Reviewed internal reports and risk assessments
  • Mapped controls against NIST CSF best practices
  • Completed a detailed checklist (technical, physical, administrative)
  • Evaluated compliance gaps in PCI DSS, GDPR, and SOC 2
  • Assigned a risk score and drafted actionable recommendations

Tools & Frameworks Used:

  • NIST Cybersecurity Framework
  • PCI DSS
  • GDPR guidelines
  • SOC 2 principles
  • Risk Assessment Methodology

Key Findings:

  • Missing encryption and access controls
  • No IDS or backup system in place
  • Over-privileged access to sensitive data
  • No disaster recovery plan
  • Partial GDPR compliance; non-compliant with PCI DSS and SOC 2

Risk Score: 8/10 (High)


Recommendations:

  • Implement encryption for all sensitive data
  • Apply access control principles: least privilege, separation of duties
  • Deploy IDS and establish backup infrastructure
  • Develop a disaster recovery and incident response plan
  • Enforce strong password policies and regular security awareness training
